About our Project

Have you ever wanted to change hospitals and wanted to transfer your health information. Well, this application allows doctors to share patient's information between each other, and even allows patients to view their health informaiton.

  • Patients will be able to look at all of their past visits

HIPAA And EDI

According to the EDI, "Data can be exchanged through serial links and peer-to-peer networks...," which our application follows, since we implemented our idea through blockchains. With blockchains, patient files will be encrypted with secure codes, and only with access codes provided to doctors can they access patient informations.
